Data recovery and forensic services:

Data Recovery

Damaged or failed hardware? Locked out of a device? Data recovery services could reunite you with the data you need. We work with any and all devices. 100% financially risk-free. There is no charge unless we can recover your data.

Forensic Services

We live in a digital world. 98% of all criminal cases now contain some element of relevant digital evidence. With the copious amount of potential sources of such evidence, examination of every source is impractical. We can help you disamibiguate all of that information to produce only the relevant and pertinent evidence and supply it to you on a silver platter. In addition to the evidence itself, we will also supply a professional forensic report of our process and findings. All forensic work is done by ceritifed experts in the field.

Investigative Services

Sometimes having all of the evidence in front of you isn't enough. Without the ability to accurately interpret that data, it is completely useless. Contact us and not only will we provide any relevant evidence to you and produce an accurate and reliable forensic report, but we will work with you to draw accurate conclusions. Our investigators are experienced and certified, and are able to assist you with the full investigative process from evidence collection and preservation to analysis.

Cyber-Intelligence

In our era of technology the endless stream of threats can be very daunting. It can be difficult and time consuming to find and hire qualified professionals, or not cost effective to hire consultants for short-term engagements. We are available to lend you cyber-security expertise on an ad hoc basis. We are available to advise or consult on any security concerns you may have. We work with teams of any size and any sector to help you understand your cyber-security risk and provide the very best expert advice on how to survive in a digital world of data breaches, adversaries, and accidents.
