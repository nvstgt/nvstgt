STEAMPoint Day for Girls: The Hunt For Señor Spacecakes

Welcome to a simulation of a very common scenario for digital investigators and digital forensic scientists. When we begin we often have very little information. Throughout an exhaustive investigative process, we gain more and more information about our subject - all of which is publicly available. This is called OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) and it's a side-effect of our digital society.

There are a lot of potential sources of information out there. Today, we will focus on one of the most reliable: Forensic Iconology.

Your mission is to employ your skills in forensic iconology to learn as much about a subject as you can in the limited time allotted.

To begin the hunt for Señor Spacecakes, head to the subject's Tumblr blog.
